DEMON SLAYER

All Hasirahs

When he first joined the Demon Slayer Corps, he only wore the regular Demon Slayer uniform but as he rose up the ranks, Kyojuro started wearing a completely white haori before donning on his signature flame-patterned one.

Gyomei Himejima

Gyomei lived with nine children who were orphans and raised them like his family in a temple. In the area where he lived, the threat of demons was very well known so he always burned wisteria incense at night but one night one of the orphans put out all the wisteria incense and invited a Demon into the temple

Obanai Iguro

Obanai was born and raised in a cell by a family who worshipped a female snake-like Demon. The Demon's face and lower half of the body took the form of a snake and loved eating babies. His family sacrificed their own children in exchange for the stolen goods of the people the Demon had killed

Sanemi Shinazugawa

Sanemi was the eldest son of seven children and lived with both of his parents. Their father was abusive, constantly lashing out at the children and their mother until one day, he was stabbed and killed by someone with a grudge against him. Afterwards, Sanemi and Genya resolved to support their mother and family

Muichirō Tokitō

Muichiro's mother died due to sickness and his father died trying to find medicine for his mother leaving Muichiro and his older twin brother, Yuichiro to fend for themselves. Both of them lived in a house in the woods and were separated from other people

Giyū Tomioka

Giyu was 13 years old when he met Sabito while training under Sakonji, and they became good friends due to their similar pasts and age. Together, they entered the Final Selection on Mount Fujikasane. Giyu was injured after a Demon's attack and was saved by Sabito, who defeated nearly every Demon on the mountain

Shinobu Kochō

Shinobu and her older sister Kanae were from a wealthy family who worked in compounding medicine but their life was ruined when a Demon attacked their home killing their parents. The sisters swore to join the Demon Slayer Corps to stop innocent people from experiencing the same

Mitsuri Kanroji

She grew up eating much more than the average person and developed an unnatural hair color (a result of eating too much sakura mochi), which led to her being seen as a freak and unable to proceed with a previously intended marriage when she was seventeen

Tengen Uzui

Tengen Uzui was raised as a shinobi alongside his eight siblings by their obsessive, cruel father. When detailing his past to Gyutaro to get Upper Six to stop gushing over him, Tengen revealed that seven of his siblings died because of their father's shinobi training

Kyojuro Rengoku

Kyojuro was the eldest son of Shinjuro Rengoku. His father was known as the Flame Hashira of the Demon Slayer Corps until his unexpected retirement. Kyojuro also had a younger brother who trained alongside him until their father ceased to teach them
